WebSpatial Widget Generator

Your Personalized Spatial Workspace

Unlock the power of spatial computing with the WebSpatial Widget Generator, a groundbreaking app designed for Apple Vision Pro. This innovative application showcases how traditional 2D web content can be effortlessly transformed into dynamic, floating 3D widgets, enhancing your productivity and bringing your digital life into your physical space.

Key Features

Immersive Multi-Window Experience

Create and manage multiple independent widgets that float seamlessly in your spatial environment, providing a rich, multi-window experience without cluttering your main applications.

Customizable Timer

A versatile floating timer for managing focus sessions, meditations, or any time-sensitive tasks. Start, pause, and reset with intuitive spatial gestures.

Real-time Weather Dashboard

Stay informed with live weather updates. Select and monitor weather for multiple cities on a customizable dashboard, integrated with real-time data.

Interactive Whiteboard (Sticky Notes)

A simple yet powerful digital canvas for capturing ideas, drawing notes, or quick reminders. Supports multiple colors and direct hand-based drawing.

Intuitive Spatial Interaction

Freely drag, resize, and reposition widgets using natural hand gestures. Widgets maintain their position and orientation as you move, ensuring a stable and immersive spatial interaction.

Powered by WebSpatial SDK

Demonstrates the cutting-edge capabilities of WebSpatial, enabling developers to build stunning spatial UI (DIV + Spatialize + Material) using familiar web technologies like HTML/CSS.
